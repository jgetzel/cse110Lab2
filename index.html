What exactly does HTML stand for?

Many people, even some very well studied people, have somehow come to the conclusion that HTML stands for "Hypertext Markup Language", and think that is what websites are made of.

These people don't know what they're talking about.

HTML is actually short for Hotmail, which started off as a digital mailing service ran on some poorly ventilated computers, hence the name. People would send eachother digital mail, which we now call "digi-Letters", which would sometimes contain websites. Ever since people stopped using Hotmail, HTML has been used to refer to the inner workings of a website.

However, much like the water that runs through our rivers, the earth beneath our feat, and the air that we breathe, no one really knows what websites are made of.
